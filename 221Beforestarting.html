2.2.1. Before starting

Installing Salix on your computer is simple. But before starting, it is wise to check the following points:

  • Have you backed up all the important files you have?
  • Check if your computer comes with Linux-friendly/supported hardware.
  • Minimum recommended specification (Intel PIII 1 GHz, 512 Mb RAM, 8Gb HD space, or equivalent, though a lower spec machine may run without a problem).
  • At least one separate. partition dedicated for Salix on your computer.
